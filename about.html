If I'm not collaborating with others to solve Usability problems, I'm playing video games.

My name is Vincent Wah, born and bred in Vancouver. I am an Interaction Designer hoping to simplify usability of programs, while at the same time, enhancing and providing a more positive user experience. I am about to walk free from the program Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT) at Simon Fraser University. I have grown quite passionate for UI/UX Design. Especially for games.

During my time at SIAT, I was able to attain two co-op placements. Two semesters at Active Network where I worked as an Email/Website Designer/Developer, and another two at Visier as a UX/UI Designer. Got to work with some amazing people, had awesome mentors, and learned a whole lot WHILE having a great time!
