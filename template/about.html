If I'm not collaborating with others to solve Usability problems, I'm playing video games.

I do enjoy being outdoors and attempting to be healthy.

My name is Vincent Wah, born and raised in Vancouver. I am an Interaction Designer hoping to simplify usability of programs, while at the same time, enhancing and providing a more positive user experience. I am currently a third year design student in the program Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT) at Simon Fraser University. I have grown quite passionate of UI/UX Design.

I love to design with team-mates because of just how much ideas can be produced in a short amount of time. I absolutely love the feeling of discussing/debating with team-mates and converging to one idea that either shoots to the moon or sparks another discussion. I'm not saying I enjoy debates and team drama, I just like the awesome ideas that are generated when having team discussions.
