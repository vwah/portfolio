Virtual Reality - Interrogation Room

A Personal Project using Google Cardboard

Synopsis:

Goal of the VR app is to test whether real life interactions can be used as a legitamite form of interaction

Two people worked on this app.

Tools/Methods used:

  • 3DS Max
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Unity 5.0
  • Google Cardboard

Role

The modelling, material applicaiton, and overall UX/UI of the project was done by me.

Learning Outcomes:

Based on a few tests and talking to people, we noticed that having the user respond via real-life actions [nodding or shaking their heads] can be inconsistent. Some individuals can nod vigoursly, others only slightly. Whether it's the limitaions of using Google Cardboard, or the fact that including some sort of UI element to assist with the interaction, we found that nodding or shaking tends to break player prescense and ulitmately, their immersion.
