Personal Brand

Target Audience?

Recruiters or managers of the position that I apply to. I will be targetting those who speak and understand the design language.

What do they need to know when visiting?

Who am I, What I do, and how I do it. Those should be the three most important things [in no particular order] when visiting the website I create

Where and how will they access that?

When entering the site, they should get a sense of who I am and my personality based on the imagery and colours I use. The content of my work should be well organized and easily a click away from the home page. When launching a project detail page, I should explain the goal of the project, how it was done, issues that came up, and how we tackled those. A scanable list of the programs and tools I used should be available.

Unique identity

A neutral grey/metallic colour scheme would be nice, but it would not stand out of the crowd. I plan to intertwine my cultural heritage and passion for games with a minimalistic approach. The trick is to balance the three so that one is not over powering the other.
The identity of games, culture and good UI/UX is still being worked on at the moment.
